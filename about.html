About us, silver jewelry

Charm Accessories online store offers minimalist silver jewelry and jewelry at affordable prices. We do not just sell jewelry, we create an image and mood.

The idea of affordable beauty in minimalism and simplicity is our anchor in the selection and creation of jewelry.

Charm Accessories offers a large selection of silver jewelry, which is an affordable and valuable jewelry material. All jewelry is made of 925 sterling silver, plated with rhodium to protect them from darkening.

Everything around us is changing rapidly, especially fashion trends. Earlier, decorations emphasized their status, importance in society. Today, jewelry or accessories for a person are primarily an element of mood, a way to complement an image. Therefore, a modern woman tends to choose original, classic or minimalist accessories and jewelry, often change them depending on the mood, time of day, season, and we are always ready to help with this.

Silver jewelry is both simple and sophisticated complementing the image harmoniously, and a successful combination of jewelry, such as silver earrings and bracelets or a pendant and a silver ring, you can easily create a new image, change your mood. There are also interesting silver sets in the Charm Accessories online store.

Our special story is unique bracelets made from natural stones . Created by the designer Charm Accessories, they have their own message - they repeat natural patterns from different parts of the world.
